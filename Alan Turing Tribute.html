Alan Turing

The pioneer of theoretical computer science and artificial intelligence

A Brief Timeline of Turing's Impact:

  • 1936: Published "On Computable Numbers," introducing the theoretical Turing machine.
  • 1939-1945: Worked at Bletchley Park, building the Bombe machine to decrypt Enigma messages.
  • 1950: Developed the "Turing Test" to determine if a machine can demonstrate human intelligence.
